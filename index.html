Who We Are

Technology education built for this community.

NkaySolutions Tech Academy was founded by a professional software developer and technology consultant with years of experience delivering digital solutions for schools, businesses, churches and NGOs across Ghana.

That same real-world expertise is brought directly into the classroom — so our students learn not just theory, but the precise skills employers and clients are paying for today.

I.
Practical First
Every lesson is hands-on. Students spend over 80% of class time at the keyboard producing real work — not transcribing notes.
II.
Community Pricing
Tuition is calibrated for Ablekuma families, with flexible weekly and bi-weekly payment plans available on request.
III.
For Every Learner
Whether you are 10 or 50, a student, jobseeker or business owner — there is a programme designed for you.
IV.
Weekend Convenient
All classes run on Saturdays — perfect for students, working professionals and parents who cannot commit to weekday training.
